Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MultiPerps uses Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data. This includes pages visited, time spent on the site, device information (browser type, screen resolution, operating system), and general geographic location (country or city level, not precise). We do not collect personal identification information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected through Google Analytics is used solely to understand how visitors use our site, identify popular features, diagnose technical issues, and improve the overall user experience. We do not use this data for targeted advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect and analyze website usage data. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to gather information about how visitors interact with our site. The data collected is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Our site uses cookies through the following mechanisms:

  • Google Analytics cookies: Used to distinguish users and generate anonymous usage statistics. These cookies collect information about how visitors use our site, including the number of visitors, the pages they visit, and the pages they came from.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our site.

5. Local Storage

MultiPerps uses your browser's local storage to save user preferences and data. This includes:

  • Price alert thresholds and configurations
  • Favorite ticker selections
  • Symbol search history
  • Dashboard layout preferences

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. Anonymous usage data collected by Google Analytics is processed by Google according to their privacy policy. Ad-related data is processed by Google AdSense according to their respective policies. No personal identification data is shared between MultiPerps and any other entity.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Control cookie settings through your browser preferences
  • Clear local storage data through your browser settings
  • Use ad-blocking extensions to limit ad-related tracking

8. Children's Privacy

MultiPerps is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@multiperps.com.
