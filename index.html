What is Multi OI?

Multi OI (Open Interest Price Indicator) classifies price vs. open interest across 30m, 1H, 4H using Robust Z-scores. Four scenarios per timeframe: Strong Uptrend, Weak Rally, Strong Downtrend, Exhaustion. Three hard filters checked in order (cold start guard, ADX trend gate, Z-score significance gate) prevent false signals. If ADX fails, Z-scores are not computed. Each TF classifies independently — no voting.

How does OI Price analysis detect market bias?

Robust Z-scores for price and OI are computed per timeframe (34-bar lookback) only if the market is trending (Wilder's ADX > 20). When |Z_Price|>1.5 and |Z_OI|>2.0, the market is classified into one of four scenarios. A composite Signal Strength (-100 to +100) with Bullish/Bearish label summarizes all timeframes. No voting — users weigh signals based on their trading style.
