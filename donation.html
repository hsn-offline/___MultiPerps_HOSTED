Support MultiPerps

MultiPerps is and will remain 100% free — no accounts, no subscriptions, no premium tiers. If you find MultiPerps useful and would like to support its ongoing development and server costs, you can make a voluntary USDT donation via BNB Smart Chain (BSC / BEP-20). Every contribution, no matter the size, helps keep MultiPerps running and improving for the entire trading community.

How to Donate

  1. Open your wallet: Use MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or any BSC-compatible wallet.
  2. Select BNB Smart Chain (BSC): Make sure your wallet is connected to the BSC network, not Ethereum mainnet.
  3. Send USDT (BEP-20): Paste the wallet address above and send any amount of USDT. BEP-20 USDT only — do not send ERC-20 or TRC-20 USDT.
  4. Confirm the transaction: Double-check the address before confirming. Transactions on blockchain are irreversible.

Important Notes

  • Only BEP-20 USDT: Sending other tokens or using the wrong network may result in permanent loss of funds.
  • No refunds: Donations are voluntary and non-refundable. They do not grant any special privileges, features, or access.
  • Not financial advice: Always verify wallet addresses independently. MultiPerps is not responsible for funds sent to incorrect addresses.

Other Ways to Support
