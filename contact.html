Contact Us

We value your feedback, questions, and suggestions. Whether you have a feature request, found a bug, or need help using MultiPerps, we would love to hear from you. Our team reviews every message and strives to respond within 48 hours during business days.

Get in Touch

The fastest way to reach us is via email. For general inquiries, feature requests, bug reports, or partnership opportunities, please contact us at:

support@multiperps.com

What We Can Help With

  • Feature Requests: Have an idea for a new feature or improvement? We actively develop MultiPerps based on user feedback and would love to hear your suggestions for making the dashboard even more useful.
  • Bug Reports: If something is not working as expected — a chart not loading, an alert not firing, or data appearing incorrect — please let us know. Include your browser name and version, the ticker symbol you were viewing, and a description of the issue so we can investigate quickly.
  • General Questions: Questions about how MultiPerps works, what data sources we use, or how to get the most out of the dashboard? We are happy to help.
  • Advertising & Partnerships: For advertising inquiries or partnership opportunities, please reach out to the email above with "Partnership" in the subject line.
  • Privacy & Data: For any questions about how we handle data, your privacy rights, or requests to delete local storage data, please refer to our Privacy Policy or contact us directly.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days (Monday through Friday, UTC). During periods of high volume, responses may take slightly longer. Urgent matters such as security vulnerabilities or data accuracy issues are prioritized and typically addressed within 24 hours.

Follow Us

Stay updated with new features, tips, and market insights by following us on X (Twitter) @MultiPerps. We regularly post updates about new features, exchange announcements, and trading tips that help you get the most out of MultiPerps.
