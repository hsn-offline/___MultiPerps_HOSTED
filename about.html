About MultiPerps

MultiPerps is a free, real-time Binance USDT-M Futures dashboard built for traders who want instant, multi-timeframe visibility into the perpetual contracts market. Designed with simplicity and speed in mind, MultiPerps streams live candlestick data across nine timeframes simultaneously — from 1-minute to monthly — so you never have to switch tabs to see the bigger picture.

Why MultiPerps Exists

Most crypto charting platforms force you into a single-timeframe view or require paid subscriptions for real-time data. MultiPerps was created to solve this problem: a single, distraction-free screen where every timeframe updates in real time via Binance WebSocket feeds, at zero cost and with zero sign-up friction. Whether you are a scalper watching the 1-minute chart or a swing trader monitoring daily candles, MultiPerps puts all the data on one screen.

Key Features

  • Multi-Timeframe Charts: View 1m, 5m, 15m, 30m, 1h, 4h, daily, weekly, and monthly candlestick charts simultaneously, all updating in real time.
  • Multi OI — Open Interest Price Indicator: See what's really driving a price move — not just the candles, but the positioning behind them. Multi OI tracks the relationship between price and open interest across three timeframes (30m, 1H, 4H) and classifies the market into four easy-to-read scenarios:
    • Strong Uptrend: New money pushing price up — bias: Long
    • Weak Rally: Price rising but losing steam — bias: Careful Long
    • Strong Downtrend: Fresh short selling driving price down — bias: Short
    • Exhaustion: Positions closing, move may be ending — bias: Watch for Reversal
    A Composite Signal Score summarizes the overall directional lean across all three timeframes at a glance (Bullish Scenario, Bearish Scenario, or No Clear Scenario). Built-in noise filters prevent false signals by only classifying when the data is sufficient and the market is trending. Sparklines (Score, Price) provide visual soft context at your discretion. All data is live from Binance with real-time WebSocket updates.
  • Multi-Type Alerts: Set price alerts or funding rate alerts with audio notifications and visual popups. Each of the four alert slots supports any alert type — mix and match as needed. All alerts persist across browser sessions using local storage and fire in real time via Binance WebSocket feeds.
  • Market Rankings: Track top volume, top gainers, top losers, open interest leaders, and funding rate extremes across all Binance USDT-M perpetual contracts.
  • Multi-Ticker Dashboard: Monitor up to four tickers side by side on the same screen for instant comparison.
  • Share Charts: Generate shareable chart images with a single click, perfect for social media or trading communities.
  • Privacy-Respecting: We use Google Analytics for site improvement only. Your alert data, preferences, and trading activity are stored locally in your browser — never sold or shared.

Data Source

All market data is sourced directly from the Binance Futures API via real-time WebSocket connections (wss://fstream.binance.com) and REST endpoints (https://fapi.binance.com). MultiPerps does not store, modify, or delay any market data. Prices, open interest, and funding rates reflect exactly what Binance reports, in real time.

Open and Free

MultiPerps is and will remain 100% free. There are no accounts, no subscriptions, and no premium tiers. The project is supported by non-intrusive advertising. We believe every trader deserves access to professional-grade charting tools regardless of their budget.
